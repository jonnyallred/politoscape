ABOUT

Politoscape is a political news aggregator that places articles on the political spectrum from the left to the right. People are forced into filter bubbles on the web, preventing people from exploring different ideas. We want to help people burst from this bubble and be exposed to quality ideas on the web in politics and give tools to users on the web to engage in politics.

We are based in the Harvard iLab mentored by professors from the Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. We are also recipients of the Harvard Insitute of Politics Gov 2.0 Fund.
